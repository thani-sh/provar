# 005 - Developer-First Initial Focus

## Context

Trying to serve all personas (Developers, QA, Product) simultaneously can lead to a diluted product experience early on.

## Decision

The initial focus (Phase 1) will be on empowering **individual developers** to create and execute tests within their local environments and CI/CD pipelines.

## Consequences

- Faster iteration and feedback from technical users.
- Robust CLI and local tooling as a foundation.
- Clear roadmap for expanding to self-hosted and cloud collaboration later.
