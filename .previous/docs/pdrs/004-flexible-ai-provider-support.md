# 004 - Flexible AI Provider Support

## Context

Different teams have different requirements for AI cost, performance, and privacy.

## Decision

Provar will support **flexible AI providers**, allowing users to choose between local privacy-first models or powerful cloud providers (Codex, Claude Code, Copilot, Gemini, ...).

## Consequences

- Flexibility to balance cost and capability.
- Support for offline or air-gapped environments via local models.
- Future-proofing against changes in the AI provider landscape.
