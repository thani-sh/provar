# 001 - Visual AI-Powered Testing Vision

## Context

E2E testing is traditionally complex, brittle, and time-consuming to maintain, leading to gaps in quality coverage.

## Decision

Provar will focus on a **visual, AI-powered** approach to E2E testing, making it as intuitive as drawing a flowchart while using AI to handle the underlying technical complexity.

## Consequences

- Lower barrier to entry for non-technical stakeholders.
- Reduced manual effort in writing and maintaining test scripts.
- Shift in focus from "how to test" to "what to test".
